Delete Your Account – Attendify - Teacher

Developer: Muhammad Usman

This page allows you to request deletion of your account and all associated data used in the Smart Attendance System app.

How to Request Account Deletion

To delete your account, please email us at your-email@example.com with the subject line “Account Deletion Request”.

Make sure to include the email address you used to sign up in the app.

What Data Will Be Deleted

  • Your Firebase Authentication account (email and password)
  • Your attendance records and any associated data

What Data Is Retained

We do not retain any user data after account deletion unless legally required. All user data is permanently deleted from our Firebase servers within 7 days of your request.

Need Help?

If you have questions, feel free to contact us at your-email@example.com.
